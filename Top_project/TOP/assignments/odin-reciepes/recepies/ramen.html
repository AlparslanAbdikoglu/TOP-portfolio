Ramen Recepie

Description:

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 10 mins Total: 20 mins Servings: 1 Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cubed cooked chicken
  • â…› teaspoon dried minced onion, or to taste 4 pinches Creole seasoning
  • 1 dash hot sauce 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles with seasoning packet
  • 1 slice pepper Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Pour water into a medium saucepan.
  2. Add chicken, onion, Creole seasoning, and hot sauce.
  3. Bring mixture to a rolling boil.
  4. Add noodles and reduce heat to medium; cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the included noodle seasoning packet and pepper Jack cheese.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving.
  7. Itadakimasu
